Mainframe Window.

The Mainframe window shows the playing table as the play progresses during the auction and play phases of the play. Below is shown an example of how it looks in the auction phase. The auction is displayed on the right side of the screen and the cards in the center of the screen. The auction dialog where the next bid is chosen is shown on top of the mainframe window in the upper left part of the screen.





You can obtain help to the next bid or play by choosing "Hint" from the "Game" menu or by pressing the space bar. The computer will then show you how it would bid or play. If you hover the mouse over the bids shown on the right part of the screen, then a pop up will show you the rules for the bid and whether there was an alert for this bid. It is also possible to restart the bid or the play or to undo a single bid or play by using "Rebid", "Replay" or "Undo" from the "Game" menu.
