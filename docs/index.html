ZBridge

ZBridge enables user(s) to play bridge on one or more computers.

The bridge table with the cards are shown with one hand on each of sides. On the right part of the window are relevant information displayed. Like board id, vulnerability, dealer and the auction in the bidding phase. In this phase is also shown the next bid pad. Here shown in the upper left corner of the window. In the play phase is bid information not show, instead is information related to the playing phase shown. Like declarer, contract and number of plays taken.
