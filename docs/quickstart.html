Quick Start

If you already know the game of Bridge, here are the minimum instructions necessary to get you started playing Easy Bridge.

Playing a Practice Hand

When you start ZBridge the first time, you are in Practice Mode.  You can select “New Session” from the menu or from the toolbar to deal a new hand, and you will automatically be taken through the bidding and card play stages.  Then upon completion of card play, your duplicate scoring will be shown and a new hand will be dealt.

Playing a Rubber

If you want to play a rubber match, you must select Options from the menu and then Game to show the Game dialog. In the Game dialog you select the "Scoring" tab and then "Rubber Bridge". This will start a scored rubber match.  Hands will be dealt and played until one side wins the rubber, at which point the total score will be tallied up.
You can check the rubber score at any time by pressing F12 or selecting “Score” from the menu.

Playing against the computer.

The score dialog mentioned above in "Playing a Rubber" has two more choices you can select if you want to compare your play with what the computer would do. One is with International Match Point (IMP) scoring and the other is with Match Point (MP) scoring. At the end of the play the scoretable shows your play against how the computer would play the cards.

Re-Deal / Re-Bid / Re-Play

You can always deal a new hand by pressing F1, regardless of which mode you are in.  You can also return to the start of bidding with the current hand by pressing F3.  In addition, pressing F4 will return you to the start of card play (i.e., just before the first trick). These choices can also be selected from the menu or from the toolbar.

Options

You can select various options from the "Options" menu to change bidding and play options.  Experiment with different settings, and when you are in the dialogs you can use What's This help to get an explanation of what the various settings are for.

Save/Load

You can save the current hand and load saved hands using the “File” menu.
