Score Dialogs.


The Score dialogs shows how your scoring has been in the game you are playing.

Duplicate Bridge.

The dialogs shown below are show for Match Point (MP) scoring, but they are similar for International Match Point (IMP) Scoring.




The first level (shown above) shows the played scores for the boards played untill now in the game. The "Board" column shows how well this board has been played in percent. The "All" column shows how well the game has been played until now also in percent. The percents are relative to the other players who have played the game.


If you click in the index column (the leftmost column) then the play for the index you have chosen will be shown.




You can now examine the bidding and the play step by step for the play you have chosen. In the lower right corner of the window you can press the button "DD Results". This will show the Double Dummy results for the board.



If you click the Board column for one of the boards in the Played scores dialog then the results for this board will be shown.


This dialog shows how the players has played this board. The Point column shows the relative scores in percent (compared to the other players) for the players from the declarer point of view. If you click the index column then the play for the index you have chosen will be shown. Just like it was shown above for your own plays, but now for the players you have chosen. Note however that the PBN files some times only has partially games stored.


If you click the All column in the Played Scores dialog then the summed result until the index you click will be shown.



The dialog shows the players relative result in percent compared to the other pairs.


Rubber Bridge.

The played score dialog is similar to the one shown for duplicate bridge.




If you click the index column you will be shown the play window as above for duplicate bridge.
Accumulated 100 points are shown with red in the dialog. If you click in the NS or EW column the well known We/They score sheet will be shown.




Sides reaching 100 point score are marked with red.
