Laying Out Cards Manually.

The Card Layout Dialog lets you quickly place cards in players hands during card layout mode. Click on a player in the top row of the dialog, then check or uncheck card boxes to assign or clear cards from that player's hand. You can also press "Clear Deal" to clear all the cards in all hands. Or you can press "Deal Remaining" to deal remaining cards randomly.


You can select the dealer and the vulnerability in the "Dealer" box and the "Vulnerability" box. It is possible to type more than one board. The "Select" box helps you with that.
